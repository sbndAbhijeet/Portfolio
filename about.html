About Me

Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Abhijeet Sapparapu, a Computer Science undergraduate at Indian Institute of Information Technology Vadodara, currently based in Hyderabad, India. I focus on building backend systems and real-world AI applications rather than purely front-end or design work.

Most of my recent work sits at the intersection of FastAPI / Django backends and GenAI / RAG pipelines powered by LangChain, LangGraph, Qdrant and commercial LLM APIs. I enjoy taking an idea from a rough problem statement to a deployed API or application that people can actually use.

I treat large language models as tools inside a system: wiring them to vector stores, web/PDF data, and existing services, and then exposing this functionality through clean, well-documented APIs and simple UIs. My goal is to build scalable, deployable AI products, not toy demos.

Alongside this, I continue to strengthen my core CS foundations in data structures, algorithms, and databases, and regularly practice on coding platforms to keep my problem‑solving sharp.
